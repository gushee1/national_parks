What is National Parks 101?

National Parks 101 is an immersive educational experience that encourages players to safely explore our National Parks. Players can hike three National Park trails while learning about the wildlife and rules of the park. From using bear spray to staying on the trails, National Parks 101 allows players to practice the skills they'll need to protect themselves and the park before stepping foot on the trail.
